====================== 
HOW TO GET A MAC APP ? 
====================== 
 
1. Get a JAR (either you compile it or you download it). 
2. Download jar2app (https://github.com/Jorl17/jar2app). 
3. Run the following command in the jar2app folder : 
   py jar2app.py "<Location of the JAR>" "SkyDocs" -b "fr.skyost.skydocs.SkyDocs" -d "SkyDocs" -n "SkyDocs v0.7.4 Beta" -i "icon.icns" -v "v0.7.4 Beta" -c "This product is licensed under GNU GPL v3. Please check https://skydocs.skyost.eu for more information."
