======================
HOW TO GET A MAC APP ?
======================

1. Get a JAR (either you compile it or you download it).
2. Download jar2app (https://github.com/Jorl17/jar2app).
3. Run the following command in the jar2app folder :
   jar2app "<Location of the JAR>" "SkyDocs" -b "fr.skyost.skydocs.SkyDocs" -d "SkyDocs" -n "SkyDocs v0.5.2 Beta" -i "icon.icns" -v "v0.5.2 Beta" -c "This product is licensed under GNU GPL v3. Please check https://skydocs.skyost.eu for more informations." -u "r6a8"
